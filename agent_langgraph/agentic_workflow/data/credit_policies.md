## General Eligibility Rules

1. **Vehicle Age Limit:** Vehicles older than the maximum allowed age for your employment category are not eligible for financing.

2. **Debt Burden Ratio (DBR):** The monthly installment cannot exceed the maximum DBR percentage of your monthly income.

3. **Income Verification:** Income must be verifiable through bank statements or employment letter.

4. **Vehicle Valuation:** The vehicle price is taken from the marketplace listing. Final approval may require a physical inspection.

5. **Documentation Requirements:**
   - Valid National ID
   - Proof of income (salary slip or bank statement)
   - Proof of residence (utility bill)
   - Vehicle inspection report (for used cars)
